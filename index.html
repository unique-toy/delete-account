Delete Your Unique Toy Account

Last Updated: July 2026

At Unique Toy, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your account. If you no longer wish to use the application, you can permanently delete your account directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Unique Toy app.
  2. Log in using your registered mobile number.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your request.
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted.
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your profile information
  • Mobile number
  • Account details
  • Saved addresses
  • Shopping cart information
  • Order history associated with your account (unless required to be retained by law)

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited period if required for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security investigations
  • Business record keeping required by applicable law

Any retained information will only be kept for the minimum period required by applicable laws and will not be used for marketing purposes.
